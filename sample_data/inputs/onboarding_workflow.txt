The HR team discussed improving the onboarding process for new employees.

Current process:
- A recruiter marks a candidate as hired.
- HR sends the new hire a welcome email and collects required documents.
- IT creates system accounts and assigns hardware.
- The hiring manager prepares a first-week schedule.
- HR confirms that all onboarding tasks are complete.

Pain points:
- Tasks are tracked across email and spreadsheets.
- IT is sometimes notified late.
- Managers do not always know when onboarding tasks are incomplete.
- Leadership wants visibility into onboarding status by department.

Potential requirements mentioned:
- A centralized onboarding tracker
- Task assignments for HR, IT, and hiring managers
- Notifications when tasks are overdue
- Dashboard for onboarding status and bottlenecks
